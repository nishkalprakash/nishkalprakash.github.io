About Alacrity V

   Hi, On behalf of Surendra Institute of Engineering & Management, Siliguri we hereby take immense pleasure to welcome you to the 5th edition of our annual cultural festival, ALACRITY V. Alacrity, which comes from the Latin word alacritas. Though quite younger to most established festivals in the country, Alacrity receives immense participation from North East region. Alacrity provides the perfect platform to budding artists to show their mettle and talent. Alacrity brings to you two days of exaltation and promises to have you forget all your worries through its exhilarating rock shows, liberating classical performances, modish fashion shows, brain-storming quizzes and lots more. Besides this you can also participate in the techinal events organized by the respective branches of our college and much more such events are a part of Alacrity V. We welcome you, whole heartedly, to our festival, where we shred all our furtive shackles, celebrate the juvenile, intrepid spirit in each one of us and raise a toast to the leitmotifs of Psychedelic life. Without you, this celebration of Alacrity would be incomplete.
