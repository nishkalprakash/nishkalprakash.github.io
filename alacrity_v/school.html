VIEW RULES

Dance Rule

1. No Entry Fee for participation.
2. Participation can be both solo or group.
3. Age limit 17 and above.
4. The use of props and costumes will be included in the judging criteria.
5. The music for the prelims must also be submitted separately to the organizers.
6. Any pre-recorded or live music can be used during the performance.
7. Participating team are at liberty to choose their own theme/form of dance.
8. Maximum time limit: 5 minutes.
9. Only one group per institute is allowed.
10. The song should be submitted in a pen-drive beforehand to the co-odinators.
11. Vulgar acts may lead to immediate disqualification.
12. NO FIRE STUNTS, it may lead to immediate disqualification.

Quiz

When? What? How? Where? Some of us have the answer, some of us don’t. But here, you ought to have an answer as you are bombarded with questions by the Quiz Master. So probe, investigate, and search for an answer. Come here and fight it out in the battle of knowledge and wit.

VIEW RULE

Quiz Rules

1. Each college team should consist of THREE members.
2. The Quiz will be conducted in two rounds-Prelims followed by the Mains.
3. Top 6 teams from the prelims will qualify for the mains.
4. The decision of the QUIZ MASTER will be final and binding on all participants.
5. Only 1 team per college is allowed.

Debate

"I've never in life learned anything from any man who had agreed with me." - Dudely Malone.
Let no eyes blink as you announce – “Friends, Romans, Countrymen, lend me your ears...” Present your opinion, disagree to what your opponents say and knock them down in the rebuttal.
Welcome to the War of Words!

VIEW RULES

Debate Rules

1. Debate will be conducted in 2 rounds.
2. Round 1 : Constructive speech – 4 minutes each team.(Both the members should speak within 4 minutes).
3. Round 2 : Rebuttal – 1minute for the questions and 2mins for answering.(Each team can question any one other team).
4. Topic: Would be shared with the participants 2 days prior to the finale event.
5. Whether the team will be speaking FOR or AGAINST the motion will be decided on the spot by taking Lots (Lottery).
6. Each college team should consist of 2 members only.
7. Each college can have only 1 team.
TOPIC: Specialisations should be done from Class 6.
