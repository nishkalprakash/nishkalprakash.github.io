About SIEM

Surendra Institute of Engineering and Management was established in the year 2009 under the aegis of a charitable society Bidya Bharti Foundation. It is named in the memory of (Late) Mr. Surendra Agarwal, popularly known as the Iron King of Patna.Being a businessman himself, he always valued the importance of education in a man’s life and cherished the dream of spreading education andproviding skill acquisition and enhancement opportunities to the young generations of the society.

Surendra Institute of Engineering and Management has a sprawling campus with state – of – the art classrooms and laboratories, well equipped library with a huge repository of books relating to various fields of science and engineering, seminar and conference halls, cafeteria, infirmary and separate hostels for boys and girls. The students are exposed to a rigorous, comprehensive and deep grasping of curricular content through experiential learning, most relevant to the modern technology. The institution has a highly motivated team of academicians who are in constant pursuit of learning recent developments in their respective fields. The constantly maintained student – teacher relationship helps in bringing out the latent talent of the students and fosters them to evolve as competent professionals. Surendra Institute of Engineering and Management regularly organize technical and co – curricular events in order to discover the untapped potential of the student community. It also organizes various Human Resource events in order to establish a healthy rapport with industry and to facilitate best placements in the region. Besides the regular curriculum, the institution has impressed global giants like IBM and IIT Bombay to train its students. The institution is constantly striving towards becoming a word – class organization by providing its students, quality education and a disciplined environment.

ALACRITY & DERNIER CRI

Surendra Institute of Engineering & Management (SIEM) Siliguri has been organizing its annual technical & cultural extravaganza Alacrity for the last 4 years.

• The colleges of hills and plains participated with great zeal in a flurry of events like Debate, Quiz, T-shirt painting, Face painting, Photography, Ad-mad, Sketching, Collage, Dance competition, Fashion parade, Instrumental, Rock-band, Singing competition during the fest.

• The highlights of the fest were Robo War, Live CS, Coding, Lan Gaming, Light Painting, Bridge It, Arch It, Graphic Design, Rubik Cube, Sudoku, Circuit Design I-Quiz and Technical Model Exhibition competitions under the technical category.

• The stage has been graced by behemoths of the entertainment industry like Sufi BAND RAETH, PARIKRAMA, DOWNSYNDROME, BONGEARTH, MOHIT GAUR (Indian Raw Star).
