About SIEM

Surendra Institute of Engineering and Management was established in the year 2009 under the aegis of a charitable society Bidya Bharti Foundation. It is named in the memory of (Late) Mr. Surendra Agarwal, popularly known as the Iron King of Patna.Being a businessman himself, he always valued the importance of education in a man’s life and cherished the dream of spreading education and providing skill acquisition and enhancement opportunities to the young generations of the society.

Surendra Institute of Engineering and Management has a sprawling campus with state – of – the art classrooms and laboratories, well equipped library with a huge repository of books relating to various fields of science and engineering, seminar and conference halls, cafeteria, infirmary and separate hostels for boys and girls.

About Alacrity

On behalf of Surendra Institute of Engineering & Management, Siliguri, we take immense pleasure in inviting you to the 4th edition of our annual cultural festival, ALACRITY 4G to be held this year, on 3rd and 4th March, 2016. Alacrity 4G might just be the perfect rendezvous for you and your Dream. Though quite younger to most established festivals in the country, Alacrity receives immense participation from Northeast region.

Alacrity provides the perfect platform to budding artists to show their mettle and talent. Alacrity brings to you two days of exaltation and promises to have you forget all your worries through its exhilarating rock shows, liberating classical performances, modish fashion shows, brain-storming quizzes and lots more.
