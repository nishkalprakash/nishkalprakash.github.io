Rules & REGULATIONS


1. Maximum of 3 participants from each college can join this Competition.

2. Each participant can choose only one theme from the above given themes. (Say for example if X, Y, and Z are participating from George College and if X chooses " Human Portrait (Young Faces)" as a theme then Y and Z have to choose among "Inside Your Room" and "Shadows" individually).

3. It is not necessary that there has to be three participants from a single college, You can even participate if you are a single photographer from your college.

4. The judgment will be done separately for each individual theme in the first round.

5. There will be two rounds in the competition - One being the submission through mail and other being on the spot click and submit.

6. The Judge panel will select three participant for the second round.

7. The pictures must be in high resolution, in jpeg or .jpg format and without any watermarks or borders.

8. Every participant can send a maximum of one photograph under his/her selected theme.

9. Photograph submitted by a participant should be an original work of the participant. Plagiarism shall lead to immediate disqualification.

10. All the submission shall be displayed in the exhibition to be held during the Fest.

11. The image file size should not exceed 2 MB.

12. The submission has to be done before 29th Feb 2016 in digital_arts@siemalacrity.in with Your Name, EXIF of the Image, the college you belong to and the theme you've selected.

13. A Printed copy of the mailed photograph has to be submitted to college On 4th Match, 2016. (Size : 12 X 18 inches[Normal Digital Print])

14. The print has to be tagged with Your Name, EXIF of the Image, the college you belong to and the theme you selected.

15. One can submit the image clicked from his her mobile.

Participants should e-mail us their entries to digital_arts@siemalacrity.in
