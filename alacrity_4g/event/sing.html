SINGING COMPETITION


Venue : SIEM Campus

Date : 4th March

Time : 1:30pm - 3:00pm

Coordinators:
                         Suyeta Banik -9733903886
                         Sourav Dutta -8159810960

The one who sings frightens away his ills. The one who sings their heart out knows how to express himself in purest of forms. The only thing better than singing is more singing. Showcase your passion for singing in a competion that will effortlessly take you into a journey towards your musical site.


Rules & REGULATIONS



1. The participant has to perform any one song of his/her own choice. The song may be in any language.

2. Any kind of pre-recorded music will not be allowed.

3. The maximum time limit for the song would be 5 minutes.

4. Maximum participant from each college must not exceed 3.

5. Only one chance will be given per participant.

6. Only one accompanying acoustic instrument is allowed. Every participant has to carry his/her own instrument at their own risk. The organiser will not be responsible for any losses.

7. Judging will solely be done on the basis of vocals.

8. Exceeding of time limit will result in penalty.

9. The Participants will be decided on "first come first serve" basis of registration, so kindly register as soon as possible.
