Rules & REGULATIONS



1. Total members in a team must be at least 6 and at most 14.

2. The use of props and costumes would be included in the judging criteria.

3. The music for the prelims must also be submitted separately to the organizers.

4. Any pre-recorded or live music can be used during the performance.

5. Participating team are at liberty to choose their own theme/form of dance for the competition.

6. Maximum time limit is 5 minutes.

7. Only one dance group per institute is allowed.

8. The songs should be submitted in a pen-drive beforehand to the co-ordinators.

9. Vulgar acts may lead to immediate disqualification.

10. No fire stunts to be done by the participants, which will lead to disqualification.

11. The Entry fees for this event is "Rs 500" for any number of groups from one college, and "Rs 200" per group from dance academies .

12. Maximum of 10 groups will be participating.

13. The Participants will be decided on "first come first serve" basis of registration, so kindly register as soon as possible.
