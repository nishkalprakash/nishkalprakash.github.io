Non-conventional energy Project Demonstration


Venue : SIEM Campus

Date : 3rd March

Time : 12:00pm - 1:00pm

Non conventional energy is generally defined as energy that is collected from resources which are naturally replenished on a human timescale, such as sunlight, wind, rain, tides, waves, and geothermal heat. Non conventional energy provides energy in four important areas: electricity generation, air and water heating/cooling, transportation, and rural (off-grid) energy services.
Participants have to display a project for non conventional energy source with suitable componetns and accessories.


Rules & REGULATIONS


1. The project should be prepared beforehand.

2. Participants will be have to dispaly their projects.

3. The projects will be judged on the basis of the simplicity, innovativity,practicalality and implementability of the design.

4. Should be able to describe their model to the judges.

5. The project should be within the topic.

6. The Participants will be decided on "first come first serve" basis of registration, so kindly register as soon as possible.
