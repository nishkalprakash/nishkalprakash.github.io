Rules & REGULATIONS



• A group has to consist of 18 members (9 Boys and 9 Girls).

• Teams can take the help of professionals.

• Maximum allotted time for each team is 15 minutes.

• On exceeding the time limit, negative points will be awarded.

• The teams are responsible for their own music and sequences.

• Music is to be submitted in a pen drive or CD and must contain all the music (preferably in mp3 format) beforehand.

• Water, fire and animal in any form will not be allowed on the stage or the ramp.

• The decision of the judge will be considered as the final one.

• Any form of vulgar show is prohibited.

• Only one team per college will be allowed to participate.

• Group name may be the respective college name or any other name.
