CODING


Venue : SIEM Campus

Date : 3rd March

Time : 11:00am - 1:00pm

Coordinators:
                         Chandan Chakraborty - 7407315404
                         Kunal Pareek - 9800191333

A Competition to decide who among us have the most Logical Mind.
Who among us are problem solvers.
It requires parallel thinking and a high level of imagination and proves to be an exciting battle between logicians.


Rules & REGULATIONS



1. Each individual will be given 3 questions out of which that they have to solve any TWO.
2. Questions will be given on the spot.
3. Judges decision will be final and binding.
4. Coding can be done in C, C#, C++ or Java.
5. Coding has to be done in Linux Platform, using gedit.
6. Compilation will be demonstrated at the beginning of the event by the coordinators.
7. System will be provided by the organizer.

Event Conduction:

1. This competition consists of one round.
2. The participants will be judged on the basis of Time Complexity of their code. Extra points will be awarded for proper Documentation.
3. Time allotted for the competition would be 1.5 hour.
