Alacrity 4G 2016

On behalf of Surendra Institute of Engineering & Management, Siliguri we take immense pleasure in inviting you to the 4th edition of our annual cultural festival, ALACRITY 4G to be held this year, on 3rd and 4th March, 2016. It might just be the perfect rendezvous for you and your Dream. Though quite younger to most established festivals in the country, Alacrity receives immense participation from Northeast region. Alacrity provides the perfect platform to budding artists to show their mettle and talent. Alacrity brings to you two days of exaltation and promises to have you forget all your worries through its exhilarating rock shows, liberating classical performances, modish fashion shows, brain-storming quizzes and lots more. Besides this you can also show your technical skills through Dernier Cri 4G, a part of Alacrity 4G. We invite you, whole-heartedly, to our festival, where we shred all our furtive shackles, celebrate the juvenile, intrepid spirit in each one of us and raise a toast to the leitmotifs of Psychedelic life.
